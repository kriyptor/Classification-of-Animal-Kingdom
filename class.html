CLASSIFICATION OF ANIMAL KINGDOM

Animal Kingdom is divided into two major categories:
    1. INVERTEBRATES-The animals which do not have a backbone
    2. VERTEBRATES- Animals which have a backbone


    INVERTEBRATES HAVE TWO CATERGORIES:
    1. MOLLUSC
    2. ARTHROPODS


    VERTEBRATES HAVE FIVE CATEROGIES:
    1. FISH
    2. AMPHIBIANS
    3. REPTILES
    4. BIRDS
    5. MAMMALS
